## Appendix: Risk Categories Defined

- **Technical**: Architecture, dependencies, scalability, technical debt, third-party integrations
- **Organizational**: Staffing, budget cuts, resource conflicts, skill gaps, organizational changes
- **Market**: Demand uncertainty, competitive threats, market timing, customer feedback
- **External**: Regulatory changes, vendor lock-in, supply chain, force majeure
- **Dependency**: Blocking goals, shared resources, cross-team coordination
